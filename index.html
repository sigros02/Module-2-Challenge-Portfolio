About Me

I am a dedicated and aspiring web developer with a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies. Proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I have experience working with databases such as SQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB, as well as server-side development using the MERN stack and template engines. My skills extend to deployment and delivery, utilizing tools like Render, GitHub Pages, and CI/CD pipelines, while incorporating testing methodologies such as unit, component, and integration testing to ensure robust and scalable applications. I am particularly passionate about leveraging APIs—whether Browser, RESTful, or JSON-based—to build seamless client-server interactions and dynamic web experiences.

Beyond technical expertise, I am committed to continuous learning and thrive in collaborative, problem-solving environments. My projects have allowed me to design and deploy responsive websites and applications, showcasing my ability to deliver user-friendly and functional solutions. With a strong grasp of contemporary web development techniques, such as modular design principles and continuous integration workflows, I am eager to contribute to innovative projects and grow within a dynamic team. My goal is to apply my skills to create impactful, scalable solutions while staying at the forefront of emerging web development trends.
